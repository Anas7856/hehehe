The Team We’re Building

We’re looking for people who live our core values:

  • Builders who take initiative
  • Servants who stay humble and help others
  • Learners who keep asking “What if?” and “Why not?”

We’re not chasing perfection or “rockstars.”

We’re looking for teammates who care — about the work, the people, and the community.

Who We’re Not a Fit For

Culture matters to us. We work best with people who lean into feedback, ownership, and growth. You might struggle here if you:

  • Need constant direction
  • Avoid tough conversations
  • Prefer to “just check the box”
  • Struggle with honesty, trust, or teamwork
  • Stir up drama instead of building others up
  • Aren’t comfortable in a faith-based business culture

Nobody’s perfect. But if these don’t resonate, we may not be your people — and that’s okay.

What Makes Us Different

We don’t just hire for skills; we hire for mindset. If you’re curious, collaborative, and aligned with our values, you’ll fit right in. From day one, we’ll fuel your growth with training, support, and the freedom to explore new ideas.
